Attractions:

There are several attractions around North Carolina worth visiting during your stay here.

  • Carowinds: The state's most popular amusement park, Carowinds features fun for all ages! Located at the border of the Carolinas, Carowinds is just south of Charlotte and is an excellent stop on your vacation! https://www.carowinds.com/
  • Beaches: North Carolina's Crystal Coast, known for their crystal waters, are serene and certainly worth a visit. Suggestions for beaches include Topsail Beach, Emerald Isle, and Kitty Hawk.
  • Mountains: North Carolina's mountains provide endless entertainment, from mountainous condos to treacherous hikes, the mountains offer entertainment for all ages and skill levels. https://www.nps.gov/grsm/index.htm
  • Waterfall Road: North Carolina has hundreds of waterfalls within it, and this useful website gives you a map taking you along some of the best that the state has to offer. This is worth a day or two-day trip on its own. https://www.onlyinyourstate.com/north-carolina/nc-waterfalls-road-trip/
  • Sporting Events: Charlotte is home to the Carolina Panthers of the NFL and the Charlotte Hornets of the NBA. Raleigh is home to the Carolina Hurricanes of the NHL. Several colleges offer great sports teams, including UNC Chapel Hill, Duke, and NC State.
  • Whitewater Center: The USNWC is dedicated to the promotion of an active lifestyle. Guests can enjoy whitewater rafting and kayaking, flatwater kayaking, paddleboarding, rock climbing, ziplines, mountain biking, live music and outdoor festivals. Visit their website here.
  • The Blue Ridge Parkway: The parkway gives visitors a breathtaking view of the mountain crests and early American settlements. All from the comfort of your car, you can go for a scenic ride through the mountains or spend a weekend at a campsite. You can visit their website at https://www.blueridgeparkway.org/
